# App Design & Unique Selling Proposition (USP)

## App Format & Physical Interaction
- **Single-Device Head-to-Head Multiplayer**: The app is designed for two players sitting opposite each other (or side-by-side) sharing a **single mobile device**. 
- **Privacy & Safety**: Because the interaction happens locally on a single shared screen, there are no concerns about online information leaking, data privacy issues for children, or talking to unverified strangers online.
- **Screen & Hardware Flexibility**: The user interface is completely responsive, adapting seamlessly to any screen size (from standard smartphones to larger tablets).

## Core Interaction Loop
- **Ping-Pong Style Conversation**: The gameplay revolves around a turn-based, ping-pong exchange.
- **Conversation Starters**: Players choose from a selection of structured conversation starters, allowing them to start speaking and interacting in the foreign language immediately, even as absolute beginners.
- **Local Active Feedback**: Encourages direct vocal communication, physical engagement, and immediate translation/pronunciation practice face-to-face.

## Unique Selling Proposition (USP)
- **Interactive Immersion**: Creating a local immersion bubble. By learning and communicating with another person face-to-face, users learn up to twice as fast compared to tapping on a screen alone.
- **No Solo Play (Initial Focus)**: To keep the learning loop pure and highly interactive, there is currently **no single-player mode**. Every session is cooperative and social (solo review mechanics are planned as future updates).
