# Future Roadmap & Scaling Strategy

## 1. Product Variations & Marketing Angles
To target distinct consumer niches, the app will branch into specialized versions:
- **FluentFast Kids**: Gamified, cartoon-rich interface using the purple Koala mascot to teach vocabulary to young children.
- **FluentFast Travel**: Rapid phrasebook-style modules focused on essential travel dialogues (e.g., transit, ordering, emergencies).
- **FluentFast Business**: Specialized professional terminology and corporate negotiation modules.

## 2. School Expansion ("Go Private")
- **The Rollout**: Once we achieve initial B2C scale, we will target private schools.
- **The Pitch**: FluentFast offers a conversational, interactive learning method that is far more active and efficient than traditional textbook exercises.
- **Lobbies**: Teachers can run "Go Private" sessions to coordinate local face-to-face practice in classrooms.

## 3. Business Models & Monetization
- **App Subscriptions**: Standardized software-as-a-service model:
  - **Monthly Subscription** (Casual access)
  - **Yearly Subscription** (Long-term commitment)
  - **Lifetime Access** (Power users/families)
- **Omnichannel Expansion**: Long-term potential to expand from the mobile app into physical language learning centers, academies, or physical classroom licensing.
