# Growth Concerns & Mitigation

## Core Friction Point: Two-Player Coordination
- **The Challenge**: Because the app requires two active players on a single device, users must coordinate physically to learn.
- **Mitigation Strategy**: 
  - Promote the app as a shared household activity (parents & kids, couples, roommates).
  - Structure sessions to be short and dynamic (5–10 minute quick games) so they can fit into any social setting.
  - Future roadmap includes a solo review/practice mode to keep individual users engaged when a partner is unavailable.

## User Acquisition & Market Awareness
- **The Challenge**: Bypassing the massive marketing budget of established players.
- **Mitigation Strategy**:
  - Focus first on high-yield, targeted digital advertising to gain a critical mass of early adopters.
  - Leverage organic sharing: because the game requires playing with someone else, every active user naturally introduces a new potential user to the platform.
