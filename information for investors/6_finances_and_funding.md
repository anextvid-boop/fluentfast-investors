# Finances & Funding Allocation

## Target Investment: £50,000
This round of funding is designed as a lean, optimized launchpad to build, test, and release the initial version of the FluentFast platform.

---

## Allocation of Funds
All capital will be deployed directly into active execution and asset development with zero corporate overhead:

1. **Hardware & Testing Arsenal**:
   - **Primary Workstation**: A high-performance laptop to handle software compilation, 3D assets, animation, and video rendering.
   - **Multi-Device Test Matrix**: A suite of 4-5 test devices (tablets and mobile phones across iOS and Android) to ensure UI responsiveness and local multiplayer stability.
2. **Software & Developer Infrastructure**:
   - Subscriptions to premium software (IDE, game engines, database hosting, Apple/Google Developer Accounts).
   - 3D/2D animation tool licenses and assets.
3. **Execution Buffer (Living Expenses)**:
   - Covers rent, food, and basic living expenses to buy 100% focused, distraction-free building time for the founder.

---

## Operating Philosophy
- **Lean Execution**: Running on a highly disciplined, shoe-string budget to prioritize product value.
- **Velocity**: Every pound spent directly accelerates the development timeline.
