# Founder Strategic Recommendations

To maximize the potential of FluentFast and optimize the path to securing the £50k funding, we recommend focusing on the following strategic areas:

---

## 1. Growth & Viral Loops (Co-Op Advantage)
- **The "Guest Conversion" Flow**: 
  - Since two people play on one device, one player is often a guest.
  - At the end of the session, offer to send the guest a summary of their performance or a "learning report card" via WhatsApp/Email. This turns the physical session into a natural lead-generation tool.
- **Micro-Sharing**:
  - Allow players to export funny or successful dialogue scripts they co-created as short audio clips or visual cards to share on social media.

---

## 2. Educational & Gamification Features
- **Speech Recognition (Zero Server Costs)**:
  - Instead of expensive server-side voice AI, leverage native iOS/Android speech-to-text APIs to verify player pronunciation locally on the device.
- **AI-Powered Scenario Generator**:
  - Integrate a lightweight prompt template using a local or low-cost LLM API to generate custom, context-specific scenarios (e.g., *"You are trying to return a broken product to a store clerk"*), keeping content fresh without manual writing.

---

## 3. B2B / Private School Entry
- **The "Pilot Class" Pack**:
  - Package a 1-week free classroom pilot with printable lesson guides for teachers.
  - Position the app as a "warm-up" activity (first 10 minutes of class) rather than a replacement for the curriculum. This lowers the friction for teachers to adopt it.

---

## 4. Fundraising Strategy
- **Video Gameplay Mockup**:
  - Since the founder has a strong background in art, 3D design, and video production, create a high-fidelity 30-second video mockup showing two people physically sitting side-by-side interacting with the app.
  - Visual proof of concept is highly convincing for angel investors.
